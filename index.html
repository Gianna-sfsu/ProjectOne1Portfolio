Welcome to My Portfolio

I'm Gianna Rosario, a Visual Communication student passionate about design.

I grew up in San Francisco, where I lived until I finished high school. After graduation, I moved to the East Bay to start my first year of college at San Francisco State University. Moving away from the city I had known my whole life was a big adjustment, but the change has been exciting and opened new opportunities. I come from a family of three brothers, and I'm the only girl, which always made for an interesting dynamic growing up. A big part of my life right now is my one-year-old Rottweiler, Champ. I'm excited about applying what I've learned to my future projects.
