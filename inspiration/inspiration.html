aaron j hartline

all of his works are very inspirational to me I would love to create illustrations like this and it

jessica walsh

she inspires me with her use of bright colors and simple designs, like in her work for the Google I/O 2025 stickers, Coolhaus brand packaging, and
Pet Plate brand packaging. i love how she combines bold colors with clean, minimal designs to create something eye-catching and fun but still professional.
her work shows me that simplicity can be powerful and encourages me to use vibrant colors and straightforward designs in my projects to make them stand out.

jeffery williams

i admire how he uses his brand to show his personality and break fashion rules.
His bold use of color and style encourages me to be more creative with my designs.
